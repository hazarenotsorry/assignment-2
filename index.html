Playing for Profit

The rising profitability of Esports

By: Hazare Renne-Glover
November 09, 2023

With the announcement of the Esports World Cup in Riyad, Saudi Arabia, electronic sports is becoming more legitimized as a source of entertainment and profit within the sports industry.

As reported in an article from Reuters, "The Esports World Cup is the natural next step in Saudi Arabia's journey to become the premier global hub for gaming and esports, offering an unmatched esports experience that pushes the boundaries of the industry," the crown prince was quoted in the statement as saying.
